# Real-Mart-Sales-Excel-Dashboard
## Objectives
* To analyze overall sales performance using dynamic, interactive Excel visualizations.
* To provide insights into customer behavior, product performance, and revenue generation.
* To help stakeholders identify high-performing cities, product lines, and payment methods.

# Dataset Used
- <a href="https://github.com/sumantgouda/Real-Mart-Sales-Excel-Dashboard/blob/main/realmart_dataset.xlsx">DataSet</a>

# Dashboard Features
## Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)
* Total Sales
* Total Profit
* Average Customer Rating
* Total Orders

  ## Interactive Filters
* City
* Branch
* Payment Method
* Product Line
* Customer Type

## Visual Insights
* Total Sales by City
* Monthly Sales Trend
* Sales vs Gross Income comparison
* Customer Type Distribution

# Tools & Skills Used
Microsoft Excel
* Pivot Tables
* Pivot Charts
* Slicers
* Conditional Formatting
* Data Cleaning & Processi

# Key Insights
* Mumbai recorded the highest total sales, followed by Hyderabad and Delhi.
* Food & Beverages is the top-performing product line, generating the highest gross income.
* Cash is the most widely used payment method, accounting for the majority of total transactions.
* Members vs Normal customers are almost equal, indicating a balanced customer base.
* Sales show consistent fluctuation month-to-month, with noticeable spikes during promotional periods.
* Average customer rating is 7.2, indicating overall positive customer satisfaction.
* Branch performance varies, with Branch C and B contributing significantly to sales volume.
* Electronic accessories and Fashion accessories also contribute strongly to revenue.
* Gross income aligns with total sales trends, showing strong correlation across product categories.
* Total 2064 orders processed during the analysis period, demonstrating healthy business activity.

# Conclusion
The Real Mart Sales Dashboard helps businesses understand performance trends, customer behavior, and revenue drivers. This dashboard can be extended with advanced Excel automation or migrated to Power BI/Tableau for deeper analytics.
